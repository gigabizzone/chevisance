## Services (4 Core + Supporting)

### 1. Air Freight Services
- Fast, Reliable & Global Air Cargo Solutions
- Works with trusted airline partners on key international trade routes
- Key features: Door-to-door delivery, defined lead times, end-to-end visibility, customs brokerage support
- Options: Urgent, standard, and custom shipping
- Benefits: Fast delivery, global carrier network, flexible options, shipment tracking, competitive pricing

### 2. Sea Freight (Ocean Freight) Services
- Reliable Global Ocean Freight Solutions
- Strategic partnerships at major container ports worldwide
- Key features: Global availability, reliable lead times, end-to-end visibility, customs brokerage, liquid cargo handling
- Capabilities:
  - **FCL** (Full Container Load)
  - **LCL** (Less than Container Load)
  - International Import & Export
  - Consolidation Services
  - Specialised Cargo Handling
  - Liquid Cargo Transportation
  - Dangerous Goods Expertise
  - Door-to-Door Logistics
  - Global Freight Management

### 3. Domestic Logistics Services
- Covers India and the Indian Subcontinent (including Bangladesh)
- Road freight + customs support + shipment tracking
- Key features: Nationwide cargo pickup from major cities/business centres, India–Bangladesh transport
- Capabilities:
  - **FTL** (Full Truck Load)
  - **LTL** (Less Than Truck Load)
  - Intercity Freight Movement
  - Commercial Cargo Handling
  - Customs Coordination
  - Shipment Tracking & Reporting

### 4. Warehouse Services
- Secure, Flexible & Cost-Effective Warehousing Solutions
- Integrates with transportation and freight forwarding
- Equipment: Trailers, reach stackers, forklifts
- Key features: Bonded warehouses, open container storage, value-added services (labelling, sticker application, packing, repacking)
- Capabilities:
  - Custom Bonded Warehousing
  - General Goods Storage
  - Open Container Storage
  - Container Handling, Transportation, Stuffing & De-Stuffing
  - Inventory Management Support
  - Agricultural Commodity Handling
  - End-to-End Distribution Support
  - Empty container storage, parking, repair, maintenance

### Supporting Services (mentioned across pages)
- Customs Clearance Support
- Supply Chain Management
- End-to-End Logistics / Door-to-Door
